Liptov

experience you need to have

“Liptov is very colorful and every day in here can be different!”

Liptov is one of the most beautiful regions in Slovakia. This region is specific with its fusion of mountains and water. Either you are looking for winter or summer vacation, Liptov is the right place for you! Rich tourism with plenty of hiking, biking, or swiming in summer or many ski resorts in winter, including also Jasna ski resort which is the only ski resort in Slovakia which can host ski world cup, it is also home slope of olympic champion Petra Vlhova.
